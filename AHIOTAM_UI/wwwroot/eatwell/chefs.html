Meet Our Expert Chefs

Passionate culinary artists dedicated to creating exceptional dining experiences.

Our Culinary Team

Meet the talented chefs who bring passion, creativity, and years of experience to every dish we serve.

Chef Marco Rodriguez

Executive Head Chef

With over 15 years of culinary experience, Chef Marco brings a unique blend of traditional techniques and modern innovation to EatWell. Trained in the finest kitchens of Italy and France, he specializes in creating memorable dining experiences that celebrate fresh, seasonal ingredients.

Chef Marco's philosophy centers around respecting the natural flavors of ingredients while adding his own creative touch. His signature dishes have earned recognition from food critics and have made EatWell a destination for food enthusiasts.

Specialties:
  • • Italian & French Cuisine
  • • Seasonal Menu Development
  • • Wine Pairing

Chef Sarah Chen

Sous Chef

Chef Sarah brings an Asian fusion flair to our kitchen with her expertise in combining Eastern and Western culinary traditions. A graduate of the Culinary Institute of America, she has worked in renowned restaurants across New York and San Francisco.

Her innovative approach to flavor combinations and presentation has elevated our menu offerings. Sarah is particularly known for her creative appetizers and her ability to transform traditional dishes into modern culinary art.

Specialties:
  • • Asian Fusion Cuisine
  • • Creative Appetizers
  • • Molecular Gastronomy

Chef Antoine Dubois

Pastry Chef

Trained in the patisseries of Paris, Chef Antoine is our master of desserts and baked goods. His artistic approach to pastry making has made our dessert menu a highlight of the EatWell dining experience.

Antoine's creations are not just desserts; they are edible works of art that perfectly balance flavor, texture, and visual appeal. His seasonal dessert menus showcase both classic French techniques and contemporary innovations.

Specialties:
  • • French Pastries
  • • Artisan Breads
  • • Chocolate Work

Chef Jake Thompson

Grill Master

Chef Jake is our expert when it comes to grilled meats and seafood. With a background in barbecue competitions and steakhouse kitchens, he knows how to bring out the best in every cut of meat.

His expertise extends beyond the grill to include smoking techniques and dry-aging processes. Jake's commitment to sourcing the finest meats and his precise cooking methods ensure that every grilled dish is cooked to perfection.

Specialties:
  • • Grilled Meats & Seafood
  • • BBQ & Smoking
  • • Meat Selection & Aging
